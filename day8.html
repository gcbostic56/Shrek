Top 5 Reasons Shrek 2 Is Better Than Shrek

  1. The Villains are better
      2. Fairy Godmother and Prince Charming are each more formidable than Lord Farquad (and taller).
  2. The Soundtrack Slaps
      3. Not to disrespect Smash Smouth, but "I Need A Hero" is an all-time bop.
  3. Better Character arcs
      4. Donkey, Fiona, Shrek, the King, and even most the side characters were given stories that allowed them to grow
  4. The stakes were much higher
      5. A great sequel raises the stakes from the original movie.
  5. The Adult Humor
      6. Scenes such as the "Knights" parody of "Cops" and Donkey being arrested for police brutality was commentary on our society, for example. Less gross out humor.
